About This Demonstrator

  • Educational/research demonstrator — indicative EAR triage only. Not legal advice. Not an official BIS determination.
  • Runs entirely in your browser — no input data is transmitted, logged, or stored on any server.
  • Applies a 5-step EAR classification pipeline: Subject to EAR → ECCN mapping → End-use/user → Country controls → Licence indication.
  • Results carry a match status: rule_matched (CCL rule fired), no_rule_matched (no CCL rule fired — does not confirm EAR99), or flagged_for_review (human review required).
  • 3A090.a thresholds: TPP ≥ 4800 T bit-ops/s (primary) OR (TPP ≥ 1600 AND density ≥ 5.92). Cite: 15 CFR Part 774 Supp. No. 1, ECCN 3A090.a.
  • 4E091 (AI model weights) was rescinded 13 May 2025 — flagged for review only; no licence requirement asserted.
  • IDD panel: MEU and SDN entries are synthetic illustrative data. Entity List and state-owned energy entries reflect public designations but are not verified against live lists.
  • Policy mapping covers publicly published frameworks only: EAR, NIST AI RMF 1.0, ISO/IEC 27001:2022, ISO/IEC 42001:2023.
  • Covers EAR only. OFAC sanctions and ITAR require separate analysis.
